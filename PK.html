Networking Education for
Students and Professionals

Packetknows: A Dual Mode Learning Platform with
Responsive Networking Simulation aims to provide a
comprehensive educational experience in dealing with
basic configurations on network devices for individuals
interested in pursuing a career in network engineering.

Courses

Introduction to Networking

The first course in a 3-course series
designed to prepare you for entry-level
networking job. First Step Toward a
Networking Career

Switching, Routing and
Wireless Essentials

This course focuses on switching
technologies and router operations that
support small-to-medium business
networks, including wireless local area
networks (WLAN) and security concepts

Enterprise Networking,
Security and Automation

This describes the architectures and
considerations related to designing,
securing, operating, and troubleshooting
enterprise networks.
